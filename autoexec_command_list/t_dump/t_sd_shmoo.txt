Usage: sd_shmoo reg_test all <sd0 | sd2>
Usage: sd_shmoo reg_test [ClkBypass] [RxClkPol] <CmdBypass> <sd0 |sd2>
Usage: sd_shmoo reg_set [DetailDelayReg] <sd0 | sd2>
Usage: sd_shmoo maxclk_set [SDCardMaxClock]
Usage: sd_shmoo rdlatency_set [rdlatency]
Usage: sd_shmoo ds_set [2mA | 4mA | 8mA | 12mA] <sd0 | sd2>
Usage: sd_shmoo show <sd0 | sd2> -- show the driver strength, max clock and detail delay
Usage: sd_shmoo dump <sd0 | sd2> -- dump the register
 ClkBypass - clock out byapss. 1:bypass 0:not bypass
 RxClkPol - rx clock pol enable. 1:enable 0:not enable
 DetailDelayReg - the sd phy control register.
 SDCardMaxClock - the sd card max clock spped.
 Example : sd_shmoo sd0 reg_test 0 1 sd0
 Example : sd_shmoo sd0 reg_set 0x391 sd2
 Example : sd_shmoo sd0 maxclk_set 100000000 sd0
 Example : sd_shmoo sd0 dst_set 12mA sd0
